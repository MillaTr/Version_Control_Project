–sweet potato and carrot soup–

  • 4 servings

Add recipe description here...

Ingredients:

  • 1 (150 g) onion
  • 1 (500 g) sweet potato
  • 3 (300 g) carrots
  • 8 dl vegetable broth
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 dl cream
  • 200 g cream cheese
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 2-3 teaspoons ground paprika
  • ½ tablespoons honey
  • ground black pepper
  • chili flakes to taste

In addition:

  • pumpkin seeds
  • cottage cheese

Instructions:

  1. Peel and dice sweet potatoes, carrots and onions. Split the clove of garlic.
  2. Add them, water and broth cubes (check gluten free if necessary) to the pan.
  3. Bring to a boil and cook for 20 to 30 minutes, depending on the size of the pieces.
  4. Puree the soup.
  5. Add cream cheese and if wanted, thin the consistency with a milk drink.
  6. Season with spices.
  7. Serve the soup with cottage cheese and pumpkin seeds roasted in a dry pan if desired.
