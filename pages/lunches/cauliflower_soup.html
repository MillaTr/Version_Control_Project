–Creamy Cauliflower Soup–

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon Mustard
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 4 to 5 handfuls baby arugula
  • 1 cooked egg
  • 6 ounces oil-packed tuna
  • 2 to 4 cherry tomatoes
    • 2 head of cauliflower 4 tablespoon olive oil 2 medium white onion, peeled and diced 10 cloves garlic, peeled and minced 6 cup Kitchen Basics Organic Vegetable Stock 4 sprigs fresh thyme 6 cup milk (I used 2%, but any kind of milk will do) 1 cup freshly-grated Parmesan cheese Kosher salt and freshly-craked black pepper optional garnishes: sauteed cauliflower florets (*see instructions below), roasted chickpeas, fresh thyme leaves, extra Parmesan, and/or extra black pepper

Instructions

  1. Make The Balsamic Vinaigrette:
  2. Whisk together lemon juice, balsamic, Dijon, and a pinch of salt and crank of pepper in a small nonreactive bowl until well combined.
  3. Add honey to taste and slowly whisk in olive oil until well combined.
  4. Taste and add more salt, pepper, or honey as desired.
  5. The dressing can be made up to 4 days in advance and stored refrigerated in an airtight container.
  6. Serve The Tuna Salad:
  7. Arrange arugula in a salad serving bowl and top with tuna,eggs and tomato quarters.
  8. Drizzle with half the dressing and serve, passing additional dressing on the side.
