GRILLED CHICKEN SALAD

  • 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 4-6 cups romaine lettuce
  • 250 g cherry tomatoes
  • 2.5 dl shredded parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 0.7 dl soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoon worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • a pinch of salt
  • a pinch of ground black pepper

In addition:

  • croutons of your choice
  • caesar dressing

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the marinade by whisking the olive oil, lemon juice, worcestershire sauce and soy sauce together with the minced garlic and lemon zest. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Pour the marinade over the chicken and let marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Cook the chicken breasts at medium heat until they are well done all the way through (approx 4-5 min per side). When ready, remove from the grill and let rest for at least 5 minutes.
  4. Roughly chop the lettuce and halve the cherry tomatoes.
  5. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces.
  6. Combine the lettuce, tomatoes, chicken and parmesan cheese together.
  7. Serve with croutons and your favourite Caesar Salad dressing.
