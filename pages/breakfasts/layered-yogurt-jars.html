–layered yogurt jars–

  • 1 serving

These easy to make yougurt jars are the perfect breakfast for a lazy weekend morning.

Ingredients:

  • 2 dl greek or turkish yogurt, unsweetened
  • 1-2dl granola of your choice
  • liquid honey

In addition:

  • blueberries
  • coconut flakes

Instructions:

  1. Add alternating layers of granola, liquid honey and yogurt into a tall glass.
  2. Serve topped with fresh blueberries and coconut flakes.
