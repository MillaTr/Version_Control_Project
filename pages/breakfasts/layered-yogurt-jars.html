LAYERED YOGURT JARS

  • 1 servings

Ingredients:

  • 2 dl greek or turkish yogurt, unsweetened
  • liquid honey
  • granola of your choice (we used Paulun's Granola with Mulberry and Cardamom)

In addition:

  • blueberries
  • coconut flakes

Instructions:

  1. Add alternating layers of granola, liquid honey and yogurt into a tall glass.
  2. Serve topped with fresh blueberries and coconut flakes.
