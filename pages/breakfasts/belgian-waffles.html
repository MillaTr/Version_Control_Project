BELGIAN WAFFLES

  • 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 5 dl all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs, yolks and whites separated
  • 1.5 dl oil
  • 5 dl milk

In addition:

  • blueberries
  • custard
  • apricots

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your waffle iron as directed.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in a bowl.
  3. Beat the egg whites until they form stiff peaks.
  4. Add egg yolks, oil and milk to the dry ingredients. Combine slowly. Stir well until very few clumps remain.
  5. Fold in the beaten egg whites. take care not to over mix.
  6. Pour the batter into your waffle iron.
  7. When the waffle is done cooking, carefully place it on a wire rack to cool, taking care not to stack the waffles on top of each other. Repeat with remaining batter.
  8. Serve waffles hot with blueberries, apricots and custard.
