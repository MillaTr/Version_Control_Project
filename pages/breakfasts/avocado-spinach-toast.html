–avocado and spinach toast–

  • 2 servings

Add recipe description here...

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 4-8 whole bread slices
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • ground black pepper
  • salt

In addition:

  • 2-4 eggs

Instructions:

  1. Smash the flesh of the avocados into a paste in a bowl. Add lemon juice to the avocado paste and mix well.
  2. Season with salt, and pepper to taste.
  3. Toast the bread slices.
  4. Add the avocado spread and spinach leaves to the toasted slices.
  5. Serve with poached or over easy eggs for a more filling breakfast.
