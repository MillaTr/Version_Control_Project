FRESH Raspberry Pear Oat Soak (G)

  • 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 c. rolled oats
  • 1⁄2 c. plain yogurt
  • 1⁄2 c. milk
  • 1 c. raspberries, plus more for garnish, divided
  • 1⁄2 tsp. vanilla
  • Honey, optional
  • 1 medium pear, sliced
  • 1⁄2 c. blackberries
  • 1⁄2 c. red grapes
  • 2 tbsp. shelled pistachios, chopped
  • A few fresh raspberries

Instructions:

  1. Measure the oats into a 4-cup storage container.
  2. In a blender or food processor, place the yogurt, milk, 1 cup raspberries, vanilla and, if desired, a couple of tablespoons of honey.
  3. Blend until very smooth.
  4. Pour over the oats and stir.
  5. Cover the container and refrigerate for at least 3 hours, or up to 4 days.

To serve in a bowl:

Spread 1 cup of oat mixture in a low bowl. Arrange fruit and pistachios on top. Drizzle with honey, if desired.Exchanges per serving: ½ milk, 2 fruit, 2 starch, ½ lean protein, 1 fat.
