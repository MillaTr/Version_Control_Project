FRESH STRAWBERRY SMOOTHIE

  • 2 servings

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups unsweetened frozen strawberries
  • 1/2 cup cranberry-raspberry juice
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1/2 cup vanilla yogurt
  • 2 large fresh strawberries, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Place strawberries in bottom of a blender or food processor fitted with a metal blade.
  3. Add cranberry-raspberry juice and orange juice and top with vanilla yogurt.
  4. Cover and immediately blend until smooth, scraping down sides if needed.
  5. Pour into glasses and garnish each with a strawberry. Serve immediately.
