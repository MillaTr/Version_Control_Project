let's cook

–black bean burger–

  • 4 burgers

Juicy vegetarian burgers

Bean steack Ingredients

  • 230 g cooked black beans (canned or self cooked)
  • 1 onion
  • 4 garlick cloves
  • 1 dl oatmeal
  • 1⁄2 dl almond flour or about 50g whole peeled almonds
  • 1⁄2 dl olive oli
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon salt
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon grounded black pepper
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon grounded paprika
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon grounded cumin seeds
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon grounded coriander seeds

Other burger ingredients

  • 4 buns
  • some lettice
  • some tomatoes
  • some mango slices

Bean steack Instructions

  1. Drain beans (canned or cooked) of excess liquid
  2. Peel and dice onion and garlic cloves (if you want you can fry them slightly on a pan with some oil)
  3. Measure oatmeal, almonds and spices to food processor, and blend them fine
  4. Add beans (leave some whole for texture), union and garlic mix, soy sauce and oil, blend until smooth
  5. Line a oven tin with baking paper
  6. Add whole beans to batter
  7. Devide batter into four and mould four steack with oiled hands, place steack to lined oven tin
  8. Bake in 200 degrees for about 15 minutes
  9. Slice buns
  10. Fill the buns with lettice, steack, some tomato and mango slices
