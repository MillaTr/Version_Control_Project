let's cook

–lasagna–

  • 10 servings

The trick of enjoying a calm and tasty dinner on a busy afternoon or evening is to make some components ready in advance.

Ingredients:

Bolognese Sauce:

  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 5–8 cloves of garlic
  • 1 medium white onion
  • 21⁄2 dl celery, finely minced
  • 800 g canned peeled whole tomatoes
  • 170 g tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons dried basil
  • 2 tablespoons dried oregano
  • 1 beef bouillon fondue
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Béchamel Sauce:

  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 dl all purpose flour
  • 1.7 l milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Other:

  • lasagna sheets
  • 250 g mozzarella cheese, shredded

In addition:

  • fresh parsley
  • 250 g mozzarella cheese, shredded

Instructions:

    Bolognese Sauce:

    1. Heat a large pot under medium heat. Add olive oil, garlic and onions. Saute until fragrant, for a minute or two, avoid browning. Then add ground beef
    2. Saute ground beef until it is no longer pink. Add carrot and celery, and saute for about one minute.
    3. Add the liquids – peeled tomatoes and tomato paste. Stir to combine. Heat it on medium high heat and let it come to a boil.
    4. Add the rest of the seasonings – basil, oregano, beef bouillon, salt and pepper. Stir to combine. Lower heat to medium heat and let it cook for another ~10 minutes, or until sauce thickens.
    5. Remove from heat and set aside.

    Béchamel Sauce:

    1. Add butter to a medium pot and heat under medium heat.
    2. Once butter is melted (avoid browning butter), add flour to the pot. Using a whisk, whisk to combine. Mixture will be slightly clumpy.
    3. Immediately add milk into the pot and bring to medium high heat so that it comes to a boil. Whisk continuously until mixture is smooth and thickens.
    4. Once the mixture reaches desired thick consistency, add salt and pepper. Stir to combine, then remove from heat.

    Assemble Lasagna:

    1. Preheat the oven to 190C.
    2. Bring a thin layer of bolognese sauce to the bottom of a deep 25cm x 35cm dish.
    3. Start layering with three sheets of rectangular lasagna sheets (see Note 1), then add 1/3 of the bolognese sauce, and then 1/3 of the béchamel sauce.
    4. Repeat Step 2 two more times. There should be a total of three sets of lasagna sheets/bolognese sauce/béchamel sauce layers.
    5. The top layer should be béchamel sauce. Sprinkle it with mozzarella cheese to top it off.
    6. Bring the casserole dish to the oven and place a cookie sheet under the casserole dish to catch any potential drippings. Bake for 45 minutes, then (optional) broil for 2 minutes to brown the top.
    7. Remove the dish from the oven and let it sit for 20 minutes before cutting into lasagna.
    8. Garnish with parsley and serve! Enjoy!
