–mozzarella pizza–

  • 6 slices

Simple gluten free pizza base and mozzarella topping for a light dinner.

Dough Ingredients:

  • 2 dl warm water
  • 25 g yeast
  • 1⁄2 tsp salts
  • 4–5 dl light gluten free flour mix
  • 1⁄2 dl olive oil

Topping Ingredients:

  • Tomato sauce of your choise
  • Mozzarella
  • Fresh basil

Instructions:

  1. Mix warm water and yeast, let yeast dissolve
  2. Mix in flour in parts, by hand. Not all flour may need to be added, depends on the flour.
  3. When dough feels good, stop adding flour and add olive oil.
  4. Pad the dough on a baking paper by hand, let it rise before adding toppins
  5. Set oven to heat to 250 degrees (heat the baking tin also in oven tin)
  6. , add the toppings.
  7. Slide the baking paper with the pizza on it to the hot open tin.
  8. Bake in the oven 8–10 min
