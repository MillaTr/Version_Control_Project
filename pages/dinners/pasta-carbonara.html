PASTA CARBONARA

  • 6 servings

Ingredients:

  • 500 g fettuccine
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium white onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 21⁄2 dl cream
  • 4 eggs
  • 11⁄2 dl Romano cheese, grated
  • 21⁄2 dl bacon crumbles
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon salt
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon crushed black pepper

In addition:

  • fresh parsley

Instructions:

  • Cook pasta in a saucepan until tender, about 10 to 12 minutes. Drain then place cooked pasta in a large bowl. Set aside.
  • In a large skillet cook olive oil, onion, and minced garlic over medium-high heat until onions are tender, about 3 to 4 minutes. Use a spatula to scrape all this into the bowl of noodles.
  • In a small saucepan, heat heavy cream over low heat until it starts steaming, not simmering as you don’t want this too hot.
  • Scoop 3 tablespoons of heated cream into the bowl with beaten eggs and whisk until well mixed.
  • Whisk eggs into the cream in a pot. Stir this constantly, still heating over the lowest heat, until it reaches a temperature of 70C.
  • Pour over the noodle mixture immediately, don’t let the mixture sit in the saucepan longer or the eggs will start to scramble. Toss to mix.
  • Sprinkle Romano cheese, bacon, salt, and pepper over top and toss to mix. Sprinkle minced parsley over top before serving.
  • Serve hot.
