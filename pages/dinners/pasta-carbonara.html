–pasta carbonara–

  • 6 servings

The pasta carbonara is a favorite recipe in many countries.It is an easily customized to the tastes of the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 500 g fettuccine
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium white onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 21⁄2 dl cream
  • 4 eggs
  • 11⁄2 dl Romano cheese, grated
  • 21⁄2 dl bacon crumbles
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon salt
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon crushed black pepper

In addition:

  • fresh parsley

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta in a saucepan until tender, about 10 to 12 minutes. Drain then place cooked pasta in a large bowl. Set aside.
  2. In a large skillet cook olive oil, onion, and minced garlic over medium-high heat until onions are tender, about 3 to 4 minutes. Use a spatula to scrape all this into the bowl of noodles.
  3. In a small saucepan, heat heavy cream over low heat until it starts steaming, not simmering as you don’t want this too hot.
  4. Scoop 3 tablespoons of heated cream into the bowl with beaten eggs and whisk until well mixed.
  5. Whisk eggs into the cream in a pot. Stir this constantly, still heating over the lowest heat, until it reaches a temperature of 70C.
  6. Pour over the noodle mixture immediately, don’t let the mixture sit in the saucepan longer or the eggs will start to scramble. Toss to mix.
  7. Sprinkle Romano cheese, bacon, salt, and pepper over top and toss to mix. Sprinkle minced parsley over top before serving.
  8. Serve hot.
