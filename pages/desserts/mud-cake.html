–mud cake–

  • 12 servings

It's been said that dark cholate is good for healt, this cake is at least good for the soul

Ingredients:

  • 200 g butter *)
  • 200 g dark chocolate
  • 21⁄2 dl sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 dl almond flour or oat flour
  • 4 eggs

Instructions:

  1. Pre heat Owen to 200 Celsius
  2. Measure butter and chopped dark chocolate to a kettle, melt with medium heat. Can also be melted in micro oven.
  3. Take kettle from stove, mix in sugar salt and flour that has baking powder mixed to it.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, mix well with hand whisk.
  5. Pour batter to buttered baking tin, 22–25 cm diameter.
  6. Bake about 20 minutes. Surface should look baked, inside should be a little sticky.
