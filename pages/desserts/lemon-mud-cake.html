LEMON MUD CAKE (G)

  • 8 servings

Ingredients:

  • 150 g butter
  • 2.5 dl of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla flavor
  • 1 lemon/lime peel (zest)
  • 2 tablespoons lemon/lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons lemon curd
  • 2.5 dl of wheat flour or gluten-free flour mixture

In addition:

  • icing sugar

Instructions:

  1. Put the oven to heat to 175 degrees and line the cake tin (round or square) with baking paper. (My casserole is 18 x 28 cm.)
  2. Melt the butter and leave to cool.
  3. Measure the sugar, eggs, vanilla, lemon juice and peel and lemon curd into a bowl. Pour in the last melted cooled butter and stir quickly.
  4. Add the flour and mix only until the dough is smooth.
  5. Pour the batter into the pan and bake in the middle of the oven for about 20-25 minutes until the mud cake looks ripe but is still soft in the middle. I baked mine for about 23 minutes.
  6. Cool the mud cake in the baking pan for a couple of hours.
  7. Garnish the cooled cake by sifting icing sugar over it and squeezing whipped cream on top.

Tip! The taste of this cake also deepens over time. Put the cake in the fridge and serve until the next day.
