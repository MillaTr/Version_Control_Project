–chocolate muffins–

  • 9 servings

Cute like a cup cake and minty fresh. And gluten free, so practically health food

Muffin Ingredients:

  • 175 g gluten free self-raising flour
  • 1 teaspoon gluten free baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 125 g sugar
  • 125 g butter (softened)
  • 2 eggs
  • 100 g dark chocolate chunks (or chocolate chips)
  • 1 tablespoon milk

Cream Cheese Frosting Ingredients:

  • 4 ounces cream cheese, room temperature
  • 4 tablespoons butter, room temperature
  • 1⁄4 to 1⁄2 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • 11⁄2 cups powdered sugar
  • Fresh mint for garnish

Muffin Instructions:

  1. Set the oven to 180Â°C. Line a muffin tray with 9 paper muffin cases.
  2. Place the flour, cocoa powder and baking powder in a bowl.
  3. In another bowl, beat the butter and sugar together until light and creamy. Gradually beat in the eggs, then stir in the flour mixture, chocolate chips and milk.
  4. Divide the mixture between the baking cases and bake for about 20 minutes or until the muffins are just firm to the touch.
  5. Allow to cool.

Cream Cheese Frosting Instructions:

  1. In an electric mixer, beat the butter and cream cheese at medium speed until creamy. Beat in mint extract.
  2. Slowly add in the powdered sugar on low speed, stopping every now and then to scrape down the sides of the bowl, until light and fluffy. Taste and add more mint extract if it isn't minty enough for you, or powdered sugar if it isn't sweet enough for you.
