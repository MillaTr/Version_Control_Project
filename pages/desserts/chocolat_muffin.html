–Chocolate muffins–

Glutein free

Ingredients

  • 200 g butter *)
  • 200 g dark chocolate
  • 2 1/2 dl sugar
  • 1 tl baking powder
  • 1/4 tl salt
  • 1 dl almond flour or oat flour
  • 4 eggs

Instructions

  • Pre heat Owen to 200 Celsius
  • Measure butter and chopped dark chocolate to a kettle, melt with medium heat. Can also be melted in micro oven.
  • Take kettle from stove, mix in sugar salt and flour that has baking powder mixed to it.
  • Add eggs one at a time, mix well with hand whisk.
  • Pour batter to buttered baking tin , 22-25 cm diameter.
  • Bake about 20 minutes. Surface should look baked, inside should be a little sticky.
